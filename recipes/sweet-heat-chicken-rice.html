Sweet Heat Chicken & Rice

Crockpot
Heat: Medium • Level 3/5
Sweet • Spicy • Buttery

Serves 5 • Cost $14.62 • Prep 10 min • Cook 3 hrs (Low)

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs
  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp butter

Directions — 3 Stages

1) Prep

  1. Add all ingredients to crockpot; stir once.

2) Cook

  1. Cook on Low 3 hrs. Avoid lifting the lid first 2 hrs.

3) Finished

  1. Rice absorbs sauce; chicken tender and glazed. Serve with peas or cornbread.

Budget Tip

Use store-brand rice and broth. Next day: turn leftovers into fried rice with frozen peas.
