Red Bean Jambalaya

Crockpot
Heat: Medium • Level 3/5
Smoky • Savory • Spicy

Serves 5 • Cost $12.60 • Prep 10 min • Cook 4 hrs (Low)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb smoked sausage (sliced)
  • 1 1/2 cups red beans (canned or soaked)
  • 1 cup diced celery, onion, and bell pepper
  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp cayenne, 1/2 tsp thyme

Directions — 3 Stages

1) Prep

  1. Rinse beans and slice sausage.
  2. Add beans, veggies, seasoning, rice, and broth to crockpot.

2) Cook

  1. Set on Low 4 hrs (or High 2 hrs). Stir once midway.

3) Finished

  1. Rice tender, beans soft, broth reduced to rich sauce. Serve hot.

Budget Tip

Swap sausage for smoked turkey to save a few dollars. Next day: add broth to make a rich Creole soup.
