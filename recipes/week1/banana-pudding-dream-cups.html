Banana Pudding Dream Cups (No‑Bake)

❄️ Fridge
Heat: Level 0/5
Creamy • Sweet • Vanilla

Serves 8 • Cost $6.80 • Prep 10 min • Cook Chill 1 hr

Ingredients

  • 1 box instant vanilla pudding
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 cup whipped topping
  • 3 bananas, sliced
  • Vanilla wafers (about 30)

Directions — 3 Stages

1) Prep

  1. Whisk pudding with milk 2 min; fold in whipped topping.
  2. Layer wafers, pudding, bananas in cups.

2) Cook

  1. Chill 1 hour to set.

3) Serve

  1. Top with crushed wafers; serve cold.
