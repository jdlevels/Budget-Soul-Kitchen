Old Roots • New Rules

Budget Soul Kitchen is a Black Southern, set‑it‑and‑forget‑it recipe collection by Jeffrey Levels designed for tight weeks and busy families. Every dish aims to feed a household for under $20 using a Crockpot, Instant Pot, stovetop, or oven — with simple prep, dependable timing, and bold flavor.

Why this exists

During tough seasons — shutdowns, overtime weeks, or end‑of‑month stretch — people still deserve warm food and dignity. These recipes prioritize low cost, minimal prep, and reliable outcomes without sacrificing that Southern comfort.
